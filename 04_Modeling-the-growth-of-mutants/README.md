Lesson 04: Modeling the growth of mutants
=======================

The purpose of this lesson is to model the growth of mutant bacteria with both the Poisson and the binomial distributions.

## Lesson Information
### Lesson Learning Outcomes
At the end of this lesson, students will be able to...
1. Develop computational simulations that model the emergence and growth of resistant bacterial mutants across multiple replicate cultures.
2. Compare observed mutant distributions to Poisson expectations and explain why stochastic mutation timing can generate highly variable outcomes among cultures.
3. Evaluate the origins of “jackpot” cultures by relating the timing of mutation events to the subsequent expansion of resistant lineages.

### Content Prerequisites

Before beginning this lesson, students are expected to be familiar with the
following content topics:

* Lesson 03: Selection-dependent mutation model
