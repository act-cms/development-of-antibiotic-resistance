Lesson 07: Timing of mutation appearance
=======================

The purpose of this lesson is to investigate how the timing of mutation events influences the distribution of resistant bacteria and to use simulation results to distinguish between spontaneous mutation and acquired resistance hypotheses.

## Lesson Information
### Lesson Learning Outcomes
At the end of this lesson, students will be able to...
1. Simulate and compare spontaneous-mutation and acquired-resistance models of antibiotic resistance using computational methods.
2. Analyze how the timing of mutation events influences the number and distribution of resistant cells observed after selection.
3. Evaluate experimental evidence by comparing the means, variances, and distributions generated by competing evolutionary models to determine which best explains the Luria–Delbrück results.
