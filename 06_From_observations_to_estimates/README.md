Lesson 06: From observations to estimates
=======================

The purpose of this lesson is to use computational simulations to estimate mutation rates, evaluate uncertainty in those estimates, and determine whether observed patterns of antibiotic resistance are more consistent with spontaneous mutation or acquired resistance models.

## Lesson Information
### Lesson Learning Outcomes
At the end of this lesson, students will be able to...
1. Infer mutation rates from fluctuation-test data without prior knowledge of the underlying parameter values.
2. Implement a grid-search approach to identify parameter values that minimize differences between observed and simulated datasets.
3. Construct approximate confidence intervals and assess the strength of evidence supporting alternative evolutionary models.

### Content Prerequisites

Before beginning this lesson, students are expected to be familiar with the
following content topics:

* Lesson 05: Spontaneous mutations
